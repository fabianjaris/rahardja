About Us

Hello, Theater Enthusiast!

Thespian Atelier is a drama production team formed by Global Prestasi Senior High School's (GPS) Class of 2026 in Bandung. The group serves as a platform for students to explore their creativity and passion for performing arts, combining talents in acting, directing, scriptwriting, stage design, music, and choreography.

Thespian Atelier focuses on creating impactful theatrical productions that highlight meaningful themes and reflect social or cultural issues. With "Sangkar Waktu" as their latest project, the team aims to blend tradition and modernity, showcasing their dedication to preserving cultural heritage while appealing to contemporary audiences. Through their work, Thespian Atelier seeks to inspire, entertain, and provoke thought among viewers.

Sangkar Waktu

Sangkar Waktu is a drama project from Thespian Atelier, the production team of GPS'26 in Bandung, which raises the theme of cultural preservation in the midst of modernization. The play tells the story of an effort to revive forgotten cultural values, delivered through a combination of compelling storytelling, music, dance, and supporting visuals. With a storyline that takes the audience through time, Sangkar Waktu invites us to understand the importance of preserving culture as part of our identity and provision for the future. Through this work, Thespian Atelier hopes that the audience can enjoy and reflect on the role of culture in modern life.
